BEACH RESORT
HABARADUWA

See why so many travelers make Navro Beach Resort their small hotel of choice when visiting Habaraduwa. Providing an ideal mix of value, comfort and convenience, it offers a budget friendly setting with an array of amenities designed for travelers like you. Rooms at Navro Beach Resort offer air conditioning providing exceptional comfort and convenience, and guests can go online with free wifi.
Baggage storage, a sun terrace, and newspaper are some of the conveniences offered at this small hotel. A pool and a lounge will also help to make your stay even more special. If you are driving to Navro Beach Resort, free parking is available. If you are looking for a nearby place to eat, Navro Beach Resort is within walking distance of some popular restaurants including Mango Mango (0.7 mi) and King Chef (0.8 mi).
We’re sure you’ll enjoy your stay at Navro Beach Resort as you experience all of the things Habaraduwa has to offer.
